Clean at Scentbird

When you spot our Scentbird Clean seal, you can be sure the fragrance has met our rigorous standards and are formulated without ingredients like: parabens, sulfates SLS and SLES, phthalates, mineral oils, formaldehydes, formaldehyde-releasing agents, retinyl palmitate, oxybenzone, coal tar, hydroquinone, triclosan, and triclocarban (to name a few).
