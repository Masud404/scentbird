After her infant daughter experienced allergic reactions to her everyday fragrance, Skylar founder Cat Chen created a clean collection of nature-inspired scents that are cruelty-free, hypoallergenic and safe for sensitive skin.

Skylar's Eau de Parfum scents go above and beyond the strictest global regulatory standards to ensure the skin's safety. All Skylar products adhere to an ingredient policy that was developed with a toxicologist to intentionally omit thousands of harmful ingredients, including the 36 worst offenders of asthma and allergies.

“I was told it was impossible, but we found a way to make clean, hypoallergenic fragrances that also smell incredible.”

— Cat Chen, Founder and CEO of Skylar
