Introducing the first fragrance from noted formal wear designer Pamella Roland: a dazzling, modern rose as breathtaking as her delicate designs. A swirl of sumptuous citrus and fruit embellishments delights then effortlessly subsides, revealing three exquisite expressions of the rose at the heart: an ode to the infinite nuances of femininity. It then softly trails off to expose an elegant, earthy essence, a sophisticated note tailor-made for standing out.
