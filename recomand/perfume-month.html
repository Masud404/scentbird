Isabelle d’Ornano developed Izia in 2017 around a unique rose. The story began with the memory of
a rose garden at her family home in Poland. She recreated this special rose which shimmers with
gold and only blooms once a year at her home in France’s Loire Valley.


This rose, known as the “d’Ornano Rose” became the starting point for a modern and multi-faceted
fragrance. Deeply personal to Isabelle, the fragrance was given her Polish nickname, Izia. Imbued
with radiant and sophisticated top notes, Izia seduces with its airy, bright floral heart.
