Join the Nest!

We're looking for great people to join our growing team.

At Scentbird, our hearts beat around delivering joy through scent and clean beauty. In addition to our direct-to-consumer subscription fragrance business, we also own Goodhabit, Deck of Scarlet, Confessions of a Rebel and Sanctuary. We surpassed our 2020 goal in the first 5 months of 2020, and we're just getting started. We have grown from a team of 3 to nearly 140+ team members across 17 different time zones. We are New Jersey-based, employing a remote-first work environment, because we believe talent isn't defined by local. We are looking for innovative, passionate, creative minds to join the Nest and can't wait to meet you.

Thanks to our people, we’re proud to have earned Great Place to Work® Certification™. Our company culture is our top priority!
